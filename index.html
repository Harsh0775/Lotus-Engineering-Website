LOTUS - Where Innovation meets dedication.

The technology behind KALYANI Mould Bases is the result of a passionate and collaborative effort spanning over three decades.

Kalyani, a mould base manufacturer with 37 years of experience, is situated in the outskirts of Mumbai. The company boasts an array of advanced CNC Milling Machines and Jig Boring Machines, all operating in a fully air-conditioned setting. These machines are capable of producing mould bases of various sizes, ranging up to 4 meters in length and 1500 mm in width. Supported by a highly skilled and committed workforce, along with a comprehensive range of backup machines including large-capacity Surface grinding machines, Kalyani is equipped to fabricate precision and critical mould bases of any specification.
